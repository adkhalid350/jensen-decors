About Us | Jensen Decors

Jensen Decors

Jensen Decors first launched in the summer of 1996, in Stockholm and New York simultaneously. Our founder, John Jensen, had a passion for redefining interior style and a vision to modernize the world of home décor — moving away from traditional, conservative designs and bringing a fresh, elegant approach.

Today, Jensen Decors has grown into a globally recognized home and lifestyle brand, offering innovative and luxurious pieces across living room, bedroom, outdoor, and office spaces.

HISTORY OF THE BRAND

Jensen Decors was first established in the summer of 1996, with roots in Stockholm and New York. Our founder, John Jensen, envisioned transforming the world of interior decoration by challenging outdated norms and introducing a fresh, elegant perspective to living spaces.

Today, Jensen Decors has evolved into a globally recognized home and lifestyle brand, offering premium and innovative decor collections for living rooms, bedrooms, offices, and outdoor environments.

Our Promise

At Jensen Decors, we promise to bring timeless elegance and unmatched quality to every space we touch. Our products are crafted with care, designed with passion, and delivered with the trust our customers have come to expect. Whether you're creating a cozy home or a refined commercial space, Jensen Decors is committed to offering innovative, stylish, and durable decor solutions that inspire.
